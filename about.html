About Ashbourne


Ashbourne,also known as Killegland, is located in Co.Meath, Ireland. Ashbourne currently has a population of 12,679. In recent years, Ashbourne has undergone great changes, transforming it from a small village to the second largest town in Meath.

Several new schemes were introduced to make improvments to the town. A number of housing estates and apartments have been built,along with several shopping streets.The town is also of close proximity to Dublin with good access routes to the major city,attracting many people to relocate to the town.

Ashbourne is famous for its well-known family theme park, Tayto Park, one of the biggest theme parks in Ireland.
