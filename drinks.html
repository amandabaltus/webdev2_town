Drinks

Although not many nightclubs can be found in the town,Ashbourne has some great bars you can go out to enjoy a couple of drinks in. Some residents favourites are Kelly's Bar and Lounge, Fogarty's Bar and Ashbourne Court Hotel.

For non-alcoholic drinks, Ashbourne has some lovely cafes serving the freshest coffee.
