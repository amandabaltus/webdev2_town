Fun

Ashbourne is home to one of Ireland's most well-known theme parks, Tayto Park. Tayto Park is filled with over 100 attractions for families to enjoy. On top of that, a zoo with a variety of animals is also found in the theme park.For more information on the theme park, visit: Tayto Park

For more local entertainment, Ashbourne has the Vue Cinema displaying the new released movies for everyone to enjoy.

Aswell as the cinema, Ashbourne provides kids with Fun Galaxy, an indoor play and party centre.

If you're more interested in doing something outdoors,Ashbourne Golf Club can satisfy your needs.
