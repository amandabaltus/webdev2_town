Eat

Ashbourne has many amazing restaurants to dine in, with a variety of cuisines, such as Fifty50, Cinnamon Garden, La Bucca, Eatzen and many more.

Aswell as the fancy restaurants, if you're looking for something more casual, Ashbourne has many great takeaway places to suit to your taste.

If you would like something light and quick, many cafes can be found in the area providing some amazing coffee and sandwiches. A small gelato parlour can also be found serving you with some lovely desserts.
